Your whole year on one screen. Meet Petals.

Petals is a macOS yearly calendar that lays out all twelve months in a single window — no scrolling. Drop images, text, stickers, and shapes onto a free-form canvas over the calendar to build your own visual year.

WHY A YEARLY VIEW
Week and month views are great for details but hide the big picture. With the whole year in front of you, deadlines, launches, trips, and milestones stay visible months ahead — and your plan becomes something you can actually see.

FEATURES
• Year grid view — see 12 months at once, with 12-month, 3-month, and 1-month zoom.
• Calendar sync — load every calendar (iCloud, Google, Exchange) as color bars.
• Event editing — drag a date to create, then view, edit, and delete in a popover.
• Decorate the canvas — place images, text, stickers, and shapes; move, resize, rotate, reorder, and adjust opacity.
• Whiteboard mode — an infinite, grid-free canvas for pure ideation.
• Themes — presets that control the background, grid lines, and accent color.
• iCloud sync — automatic across your devices.

MADE FOR
• Professionals juggling multiple projects who need a bird's-eye view of the year.
• Freelancers and creators tracking deadlines, launches, and content plans.
• Visual planners who love decorating their calendar and making it their own.
• Anyone building a vision board, tracking goals, or mapping out the new year.
